Return & Refund Policy

Everything you need to know about our exchanges and returns.

At Madamcutie, we want you to be entirely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not completely happy with your items, we offer a straightforward return and exchange process.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return or exchange, your item must meet the following criteria:

  • Initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and undamaged.
  • All original tags and labels must be firmly attached.
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging.

Non-returnable Items

Please note that for hygiene and clearance reasons, the following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Innerwear and intimate apparel.
  • Jewelry and hair accessories.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or purchased during a clearance event.

Refund Options

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, you may choose between:

  • Original Payment Method: The refund will be processed and applied to your original credit card or payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • Store Credit: Receive Madamcutie store credit instantly upon return approval, which never expires.

Exchange Process & Timelines

If you need a different size or color, an exchange is your fastest option. Here is the process:

  • Contact our support team to initiate the exchange request.
  • Once we provide the return label, ship the item back to us.
  • Upon receipt and inspection (usually takes 24-48 hours), we will ship out the replacement item.
  • Estimated total timeline for an exchange is 7-10 business days depending on your location.

Need help?

If you have any further questions regarding your specific return or exchange, our support team is ready to assist you.
